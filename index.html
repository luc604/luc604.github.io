Condor

12KG AUW - 120km/hr max speed - 806Whr Battery - 1500mm Wing span

A high speed, heavy payload aircraft capable of long range autonomous missions. Condor put my design and engineering skills to the test. Optimizations in weight, stiffness, strength, range, and speed, presented significant challenges that required in depth testing and verification to ultimately create the most advanced multi rotor UBC UAS has ever seen.

VTOL

This was a short project aimed at getting a proof of concept VTOL (Vertical Take Off and Landing) aircraft in the air as quickly as possible. For simplicity, I used an old off the shelf 1100mm flying wing I had on hand. The lift motors and ESCs were pulled from a 250mm class mini quad, paired with larger 6in props for better low speed lifting capacity. I used the stock pusher motor and prop as it was deemed sufficient. Finally a pixhawk one was installed with only the essentials carefully crammed into the small cargo bay (including a pitot tube air speed sensor. Several hover flights and partial transitions to forward flight and back to hover were achieved. Unfortunately only one full transition to forward flight was achieved while I was not present which resulted in a complete crash due to pilot error.
